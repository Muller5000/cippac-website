Service Profile 02

Trademark Advisory & Brand Protection

Securing brand names, logos, phrases, and visual commercial identities for research spin-offs and university initiatives.

Service Overview

While patents protect technological functions and processes, trademarks secure the visual identity, brand names, and slogans that distinguish university-backed spin-offs in the commercial market.

CIPPAC provides comprehensive trademark searches, helps inventors categorize products under correct international classifications (Nice Agreement), and manages registrations with the Commercial Law Department of the Federal Ministry of Industry, Trade and Investment (Nigeria).

Our Trademark Process

1. Trademark Search & Availability Review

We check national registry databases to verify that your brand name, logo, or slogan is not identical or confusingly similar to pre-existing registrations in Nigeria.

2. Classification of Goods & Services

We guide you in selecting correct Nice classes corresponding to your commercial spin-off activities (e.g., Class 5 for pharmaceuticals, Class 42 for software services).

3. Filing and Registration

CIPPAC compiles filings, submits registrations to the Commercial Law Department in Abuja, coordinates responses to oppositions, and manages trademark certificates issuance.

4. Portfolio Audits & Renewals

Trademarks in Nigeria must be renewed periodically. We track timeline dates and manage renewals to ensure your spin-off assets remain permanently protected.

What We Protect

Our brand protection advisory services cover the following components:

  • Brand Names & Slogans: Slogans, venture titles, or research lab identifiers.
  • Visual Logos & Emblems: Custom icons representing university spin-offs or products.
  • Package Design & Trade Dress: Unique visual combinations, colors, and shapes of product packaging.
