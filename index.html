University of Ibadan Innovation Ecosystem

Bridging Academic Excellence and Economic Impact

The Centre for Intellectual Property Protection and Commercialization (CIPPAC), University of Ibadan, is the official institutional engine dedicated to identifying, protecting, and commercializing the university's intellectual assets.

We empower inventors and researchers by providing advisory services, patent applications support, and licensing coordination, while actively matchmaking academy innovations with corporate industry partners to drive national economic development.

Leadership Spotlight

Guiding Intellectual Property & Commercialization at UI

Professor Oladapo Titus Okareh is a distinguished scholar and Professor of Environmental Health Sciences in the College of Medicine, University of Ibadan, Nigeria. His academic excellence was nationally recognized by the National Universities Commission (NUC) with a merit award for the best doctoral thesis in Public Health in 2008.

Re-appointed as Director of the Centre for Intellectual Property Protection and Commercialization (CIPPAC) in September 2023, Prof. Okareh has championed institutional patenting, technology licensing, and matchmaking university innovations with global markets to drive tangible socio-economic impact.
